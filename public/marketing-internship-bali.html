What you'll do

Real marketing work inside real companies.

Not observer roles. Not admin support. Marketing interns in Bali work on live campaigns and brand output from week one.

Campaign execution

Plan and run digital campaigns

Work inside teams managing paid social, email, and content campaigns with real budgets and measurable outcomes you can describe in any future interview.

Social media management

Own content channels

Manage Instagram, LinkedIn, or TikTok accounts for brands — from content calendar to post scheduling to performance analysis.

Content creation

Create brand content

Write copy, produce visuals, and support content strategy for brands that need consistent output across digital channels.

Growth & analytics

Measure and report performance

Track campaign data, build simple performance reports, and work with teams that make decisions based on what the numbers show.

What you leave with

Career proof that follows you after the internship ends.

CV proof

Portfolio and campaign deliverables

Real output — campaigns, content, reports — you can show in interviews and reference in your CV with specifics, not vague bullet points.

Business exposure

Inside a real international business

Commercial awareness built through daily work close to founders, managers, and marketing leads in an English-speaking international environment.

International network

Contacts across industries and countries

A professional and personal network that spans your co-interns, host company team, and the broader Bali international community.

Career direction

Clarity on what you actually want next

Leave knowing which marketing disciplines you enjoy, which environments you thrive in, and what kind of role you want to target after graduation.
